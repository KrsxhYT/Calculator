About Multi Calculator

Multi Calculator is an all-in-one utility web app designed for fast and accurate daily calculations. Whether you need to calculate your age, BMI, loan EMI, currency exchange, or convert units like length, speed, time, or temperature — this app covers everything in a sleek, mobile-friendly interface.

Built with passion by Krsxh, this project focuses on simplicity, speed, and offline usability. It's perfect for students, developers, and anyone needing quick answers without clutter.
